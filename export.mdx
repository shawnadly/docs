### How to export

1. Click **Export** in the footer.
2. Choose **DOCX** or **PDF**.
3. Download and review.

### Format comparison

| Format | Best for                          | Notes                                          |
| :----- | :-------------------------------- | :--------------------------------------------- |
| DOCX   | Editing in Microsoft Word         | Maintains headings and most formatting         |
| PDF    | Final fixed layout for submission | Looks the same everywhere; not easily editable |

**Tip:** If formatting looks off, try the other format or re-apply headings/lists.
