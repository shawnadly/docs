## About SparkDoc

SparkDoc is the go-to AI writing workspace for those looking to write faster and streamline their reading, drafting, and research. Designed to remove friction from writing and empower writers to ship with confidence.

## Who is it for?

Students writing assignments, literature summaries, and research papers.

Educators and professionals preparing briefs, memos, and reports. 

Anyone who wants a highly efficient, citation-aware editor with AI assist.
