### Rename a document

1. Click the title at the top of the editor.
2. Type the new name and press **Enter**.

### Find your document later

- Open **Documents** in the left sidebar to browse.
- Use search (if available) with a keyword from the title.

**Tip:** Use a prefix like _Course — Assignment — Topic_ for quick scanning.
